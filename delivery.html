How to Make a Wonderland Cocktail:
1. Put the content in the capsule into a glass
2. Add alcohol base; use the capsule as a measuring cup
(0 cups: non-alcoholic/1 cup: 3.5%/2 cups: 7.1%/3 cups:10.6%)
3. Stir the drink properly (certain flavors may be hard to stir)
4. Add ice cubes
5. Add sparkling water (4 cups: 80 ml)
Now , Order and enjoy it !
